FLAIR: Catching Bad Traffic, on the Ryzen AI NPU

FLAIR is an AI model that learns what “normal” network traffic looks like, then flags anything that doesn’t match as a possible attack. This demo runs the exact same detector two ways — once on the computer’s CPU, once on its AMD Ryzen AI NPU (a dedicated AI chip) — on the same real traffic samples, side by side.
